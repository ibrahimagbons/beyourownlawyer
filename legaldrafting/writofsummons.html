AFFIDAVIT IN SUPPORT

I, MrsKayuba Ada, Adult, Female, Christian, Business woman and a Citizen of Nigeria residing at No. 10 Ogun Street Garki 2 Abuja do hereby make oath and state as follows:

  1. I am the Plaintiff in this matter and by virtue of which I am conversant with the facts of this case.
  2. The plaintiff entered into a contract with the defendant for the supply of 5,000 tons of cashew nuts worth N10, 000, 000.00 on the 15 day of June 2000, a copy of the contract agreement is attached and marked Exhibit A.
  3. It was agreed as terms of the contract that a part payment of N3, 000,000.00 be paid as advance and the balance be paid upon the actual supply of the cashew nuts by the Plaintiff.
  4. The plaintiff has supplied the 5,000 tons of cashew nuts on the 30 of September 2000 to the defendant and the balance of N7, 000, 000.00 has not been made to the plaintiff.
  5. The plaintiff has written several Letters of demand to the defendant who has always pleaded that sometime be given to it to clear up/pay the balance, copies of the two Letter of demand and the reply from the defendant are hereby attached and marked as exhibit B, C and D respectively.
  6. That I believe the defendant has no good defence to the Plaintiff’s claims.
  7. I make this statement in good faith believing its content to be true and correct in accordance with the Oaths Act.


……………………
Deponent
